Game Design

This was the first game we made in class. A few classmates and I build a board game with a mat and pieces to place on the board. These are the pieces combineded. The game is called Bunker Rush.

This is our 2nd game my group have made during this semester. It's an ablib game similar to "Cards Against Humanity or Apples to Apples". You pick up 5 cards from the deck of 50 and choose 3 of them to use to create your story. Then the Innkeeper gets to choose which story is edgy. Whoever wins gets a Beer token for that round. Then the Innkeeper changes. The game is called What's Your Story.

Our last and final game we made in class is similar to What's Your Story, but this is like a DLC. An add on to the game known as What's Your Journey. You have a challenge deck, Action deck and setting deck. The Innkeeper flips over a challenge and a setting card. You pick up 3 cards from the Actions and choose one to play. Whoever the Innkeeper picks for the best Journey wins a treasure chest token.
